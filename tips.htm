TIPS

Minigames

Castle Fireworks Remixed

Castle Fireworks Remixed is a game about timing.

  • In the first two levels, try to not worry all too much about the combos. Instead, focus more on getting all Flawless.
  • When you start to get to get to around the third level, start going for the combos (within moderation). <-- Hover Over This - -
  • Pay attention to the Coming In section in the lower right corner. Knowing what is coming up can be very helpful.
  • Practice. Getting familiar with the minigame itself is really important if you want to get a good score. Once you've played the game quite a bit, you start to learn the patterns and order of the various seeds.

Jungle Cruise

Jungle Cruise is a game about precision and speed.

  • One of the most important thing in just about every minigame is to get to know every level. The more your know about each level, the more prepared you can be. This can be especially helpful in Jungle Cruise, since you have to quickly click on the animals with precision
  • Try to not speed up or slow down as much as possible, as this can waste some gas you might need later.
  • Make sure to get all of the film and gas. You don't want to run out of either of those thing..!
